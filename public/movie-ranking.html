The Best Movies According to Amir

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Batman Begins

A tale of redemption and fear, where Bruce Wayne finds purpose through his darkest fears to become Gotham's symbol of hope.

The Dark Knight

A thrilling saga of morality and chaos, where Batman faces the Joker's anarchic brilliance, testing Gotham's soul in a battle of ideologies.

The Dark Knight Rises

A gripping epic where Bruce Wayne confronts his physical and emotional limits, rising from despair to protect Gotham against a merciless adversary.
